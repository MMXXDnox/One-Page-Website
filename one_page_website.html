Olympic Sports No Longer on the Program

Sports That Are No Longer Included

The Olympic program has evolved over the years, with certain sports being added and removed.

  1. Croquet: Introduced in 1900, removed after that year.
  2. Jeu de Paume: Introduced in 1908, removed after that year.
  3. Roque: Introduced in 1904, removed after that year.
  4. Racquets: Introduced in 1908, removed after that year.
  5. Polo: Introduced in 1900, last included in 1936.
  6. Tug of War: Introduced in 1900, last included in 1920.
  7. Water Motor Sports: Introduced in 1906 (Intercalated Games), removed after that year.
  8. Basque Pelota: Introduced in 1900, removed after that year.
  9. Cricket: Introduced in 1900, removed after that year.
  10. Lacrosse: Introduced in 1904 and 1908, removed after 1908

Noteworthy Returns

While these sports have been removed, it's important to note that some have made a comeback in recent years or are scheduled to return:

  1. Golf: Removed after 1936, reintroduced in 2016.
  2. Rugby: Removed in 1924, reintroduced in 2016 as Rugby Sevens.
  3. Baseball/Softball: Removed after 2008, reintroduced in 2020 (combined), with separate events from 2024.
  4. Cricket: Set to return in 2028.
  5. Lacrosse: Set to return in 2028 as Lacrosse Sixes.
